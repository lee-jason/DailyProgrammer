Description

Trees have a small chance to spawn sprouts. Sprouts will grow into fully fledged Normal Trees in a year. Normal Trees will grow into an Elder Tree after 10 years

Lumberjacks roam the lands chopping wood. More lumberjacks will spawn in the map depending on need. Lumberjacks will not cut sprouts.

Bears roam the lands looking for lumberjacks. For every maw incident per year a bear will be caught and removed
